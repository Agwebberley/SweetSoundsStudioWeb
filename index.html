About Me

Hi, My name is Kristen Webberley, and I am fourteen years old. Ever since I can remember, I have had a love for Music. I have been playing piano for five years now and have loved every second. My piano teacher has inspired me to become a teacher like her. I also love children; I work in the nursery at my Church and have worked in a three-year-old classroom since six grade. My desire when I teach is to glorify my Lord and Savior. I want to inspire my students like my teacher did for me, to grow in my love for music.
